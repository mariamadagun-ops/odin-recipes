Mango Ice Cream

Description

A perfect and healthy way to start of summer. This recipe is simply and only requires a few ingredients.

You can make this if you want something delicious and cold.

Ingredients

  • Mango
  • Silken Tofu (Drained)
  • Maple Syrup or Agave
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Syrup
  • Coconut Milk

Ingredients Measurements

  • 3 cups of frozen Mango chunks
  • 1/4 cups of Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 of Silken Tofu (Drained)
  • 3 tablespoons of Maple Syrup or Agave
  • A pinch of salt
  • A teaspoon of Vanilla Syrup

Equipment

  • Blender
  • Plastic or Metal tray
  • Spatula

Instructions

  1. Add frozen mango chunks, silken tofu and coconut milk to the blender.
  2. Pulse the blender a few times then blend on high speed for 20- 30 seconds until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add the vanilla, maple or agave syrup and the pinch of salt and blend again until combined.
  4. Transfer to your plastic or metal container. You can serve as soft serve or you can put it into the freezer for 20- 30 seconds. Using the spatula to stir every 20 minutes until you get your desired consistency.

Enjoy your Mango Ice Cream!
