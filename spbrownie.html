Sweet Potato Brownies

Description

This will be a simple and short tutorial on how to make Sweet Potato Brownies.

They're are high in fibre, high in protein, gluten free and simply delicious.

This recipe is vegan and requires only a handful of ingredients and some can be found at home. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potato
  • Coco Powder or Cacao Powder
  • Unsweetened Almond Butter (Smooth)
  • Maple Syrup
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Salt

Ingredients Measurements

  • 1 1/2 cups of mashed and roasted Sweet Potato
  • 1/4 cups of Coco Powder or Cacao Powder
  • 3/4 cups of unsweetened Almond Butter (Smooth)
  • 1/4 cups of Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 cups of Chocolate Chips
  • A pinch of salt

Equipment

  • Blender or Food Processor
  • Spatula
  • 8x8 pan
  • Parchment Paper

Baking Instructions

  1. Roast the sweet potato till soft to touch.
  2. Whilst you wait for the sweet potato to cool preheat your oven to 350 fahrenheit or 170 degrees celsius.
  3. Once the sweet potato has cooled add all the following ingredients expect the chocolate chips to a blender or food processor.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly with the spatula, then add chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the centre of the brownie is firm.
  6. Remove from the oven and leave to cool entirely and cut into equal squares.

And voila, you have made yourself come Sweet Potato Brownies!
